ABOUT

Alexander Eyo is a 19 year old photographer and multi-media artist from Prince George's County Maryland. His work aims to highlight day to day life and the interesting moments it has to offer in a colorful and or unique ways. He is currently working towards an Interactive Digital Media major working towards his Bachelors degree at Drexel University.
